Chest Exercises

  • Bench Press: A compound exercise that targets the chest muscles.
  • Push-Ups: A bodyweight exercise that strengthens the chest, shoulders, and triceps.
  • Chest Fly: An isolation exercise that targets the chest muscles.

Back Exercises

  • Deadlift: A compound exercise that targets the entire back, glutes, and hamstrings.
  • Pull-Ups: A bodyweight exercise that targets the lats and upper back.
  • Bent-Over Row: A compound exercise that targets the middle back and lats.

Leg Exercises

  • Squats: A compound exercise that targets the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes.
  • Lunges: A bodyweight exercise that targets the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes.
  • Leg Press: A machine-based exercise that targets the quadriceps and glutes.

Shoulder Exercises

  • Overhead Press: A compound exercise that targets the shoulders and triceps.
  • Lateral Raise: An isolation exercise that targets the lateral deltoids.
  • Front Raise: An isolation exercise that targets the front deltoids.
